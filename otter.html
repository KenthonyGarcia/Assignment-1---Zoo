Otter

The Otters are carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae. Lutrinae is a branch of the Mustelidae family, which also includes weasels, badgers, mink, and wolverines, among other animals. The 13 extant otter species are all semiaquatic, aquatic, or marine, with diets based on fish and invertebrates. When sleeping, Sea Otters hold hands so that they do not drift apart.
